Lionel Messi

The little boy from Rosario, Argentina

Here's a time line of Lionel Messi's life:

  • 1987 - Born in Rosario, Argentina.

  • 1995 - Joins Newell's Old Boys youth team.

  • 2000 - Moves to Barcelona, Spain, to join FC Barcelona's famed La Masia academy.

  • 2003 - Makes his official debut for FC Barcelona's first team at the age of 16.

  • 2005 - Wins his first La Liga title and FIFA World Youth Championship with Argentina, where he finishes as the top scorer.

  • 2009 - Wins his first Ballon d'Or, awarded to the best player in the world, and leads Barcelona to an unprecedented sextuple (six major trophies in one year).

  • 2011 - Helps Barcelona win another Champions League title and secures his third consecutive Ballon d'Or.

  • 2012 - Sets a new record for the most goals scored in a calendar year, with 91 goals.

  • 2014 - Leads Argentina to the World Cup final, earning the Golden Ball as the tournament's best player.

  • 2015 - Wins his fourth Champions League title with Barcelona.

  • 2019 - Wins his sixth Ballon d'Or, becoming the player with the most Ballon d'Or awards in history.

  • 2021 - Leaves FC Barcelona after 21 years and signs with Paris Saint-Germain (PSG). Wins the Copa America with Argentina, his first major international trophy.

  • 2022 - Captains Argentina to victory in the FIFA World Cup, cementing his legacy as one of the greatest footballers of all time.
